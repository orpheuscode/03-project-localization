Intel's Sustainability Pillars

Three core commitments driving our path to a net-zero future.

Climate Action

Intel is committed to achieving net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across Scope 1 and 2 by 2040. We invest in renewable energy, chemical abatement, and energy-efficient manufacturing at every facility worldwide.

Learn More

Water Stewardship

Intel conserves billions of gallons of water annually through recycling and efficiency programs. We collaborate with local communities to restore watersheds and reduce freshwater consumption across our global operations.

Learn More

Waste & Circularity

Intel diverts millions of pounds of waste from landfills each year through recycling, reuse, and circular economy initiatives. We work with suppliers to minimize packaging waste and recover valuable materials from manufacturing.

Frequently Asked Questions

RISE stands for Responsible, Inclusive, Sustainable, and Enabling. Launched in 2020, it is Intel's corporate responsibility strategy that sets ambitious 2030 goals across climate action, water stewardship, waste reduction, and inclusive workforce development.

Intel has committed to achieving net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across Scope 1 and Scope 2 by 2040. This builds on the milestone of reaching 99% renewable electricity usage worldwide in 2023.

Intel conserves billions of gallons of water annually through on-site recycling systems, efficiency improvements in chip manufacturing, and partnerships with local communities to restore watersheds near its facilities.

In 2024, Intel hosted its first Sustainability Summit, bringing together suppliers, government officials, and industry leaders to collaborate on next-generation sustainable semiconductor manufacturing practices and supply chain transparency.
